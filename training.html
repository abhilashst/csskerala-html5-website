Our Training

Centre for Socio-Economic Studies is an autonomous research and training organization . It is a centre for overall public-awakening activities and research projects in applied economics and topics germane to socio-economic development of Kerala society

CSS is dedicated to providing all those involved in the research field with the opportunity to increase their knowledge and provide them with the skills to further develop their careers in research. CSS offers a comprehensive series of workshops throughout the year.
