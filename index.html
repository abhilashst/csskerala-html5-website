Welcome to Centre For Socio Economic Studies Kerala

Centre for Socio-Economic Studies is an autonomous research and training organization . It is a centre for overall public-awakening activities and research projects in applied economics and topics germane to socio-economic development of Kerala society

Our Mission

Of all the challenges that humanity faces today, one of the greatest, and possibly the gravest, is overcoming the CRISIS OF LEADERSHIP. It is due to this crisis that we are plagued with the pressing concern for a prospering present and a promising future. We provide extensive and exhaustive training programmes for the public in various fields .
