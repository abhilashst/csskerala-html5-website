Our Academic Council

Centre for Socio-Economic Studies is an autonomous research and training organization . It is a centre for overall public-awakening activities and research projects in applied economics and topics germane to socio-economic development of Kerala society

Centre for Socio-Economic Studies is founded with a motive to support admirable research works which possess immense potential to bring growth, prosperity and can ensure wellbeing of the society. Such research will be selected by our academic council after thorough review only.
