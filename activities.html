Our Activities

Centre for Socio-Economic Studies is an autonomous research and training organization. It is a centre for overall public-awakening activities and research projects in applied economics and topics germane to socio-economic development of Kerala society

CSS believes that promoting sustainable agriculture and rural development plays an important role in development of our country. We must promote researches that can help increase food production in a sustainable way and enhance food security. This will involve education initiatives, utilization of economic incentives and the development of appropriate and new technologies, thus ensuring stable supplies of nutritionally adequate food.
